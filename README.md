# AI Shortform Prompt Kit

AI Shortform Prompt Kit is an open-source collection of prompt templates, workflow guides, and production checklists for creators, marketers, and small teams who make AI-generated shortform videos, brand visuals, character concepts, and social media content.

This project is designed especially for non-developers who want to use generative AI in a more structured, repeatable, and production-ready way.

## Why This Project Exists

Many creators use AI tools for image generation, video generation, writing, and editing, but their workflow is often scattered across notes, chats, screenshots, and random prompt experiments.

AI Shortform Prompt Kit helps organize that process into reusable templates.

The goal is to make AI content production easier to plan, test, revise, and share.

## Prompt Categories

### 1. Brand Campaign Prompts

Reusable prompt structures for creating visual concepts, campaign key visuals, and social media assets based on brand mood, product features, and target audience.

### 2. Character Sheet Prompts

Templates for creating consistent AI-generated characters, including front view, side view, back view, expressions, poses, and material details.

### 3. Shortform Storyboard Prompts

Templates for planning AI shortform videos scene by scene, including visual direction, camera movement, mood, lighting, sound, and editing notes.

### 4. Product Visual Prompts

Templates for generating product-focused visuals such as lifestyle images, editorial campaign images, seasonal visuals, and commercial-style product scenes.

## Example Use Case

A small brand wants to create a 15-second AI-generated shortform video for a seasonal campaign.

Using this kit, the creator can:

1. Define the campaign concept
2. Write a short storyboard
3. Generate image or video prompts
4. Check brand consistency
5. Review the output with a production checklist
6. Revise based on client feedback

## Example Prompt

```text
Create a cinematic AI shortform video concept for a home interior paint brand.

Mood:
Fresh, bright, calm, editorial, seasonal

Product:
Interior paint for small home makeovers

Target Audience:
Young adults who want to refresh their living space without a full renovation

Video Length:
15 seconds

Structure:
Scene 1: Before mood
Scene 2: Color transformation
Scene 3: Finished space
Scene 4: Product or campaign message

Style:
Clean composition, soft natural light, subtle camera movement, magazine-like visual direction

Output:
Write a scene-by-scene storyboard with visual direction, text overlay, camera movement, and sound direction.
```
